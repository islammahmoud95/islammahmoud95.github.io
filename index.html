About

8+ years, 15+ apps shipped.

I'm a Senior Android Developer based in Beni Suef, Egypt, currently leading Android development for GoodsMart's e-commerce platform. Over 8+ years I've shipped large-scale apps across e-commerce, delivery, fintech and healthcare.

My core strength is Clean Architecture, MVVM/MVI, and Jetpack Compose, paired with hands-on experience wiring up real payment infrastructure — PayMob, HyperPay, PayTabs, Tamara, NBE, PayFort and Google Pay — across multiple production apps.

I've also focused heavily on app stability: reducing crash rates and improving performance using Firebase Crashlytics, LeakCanary, and Android Studio Profiler.
